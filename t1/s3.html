12th Fail Wikipedia

In the film "12th Fail," we follow the journey of a high school student named Rahul who faces the harsh reality of failing his 12th-grade exams. Devastated by this setback and feeling the weight of societal expectations, Rahul grapples with disappointment, self-doubt, and familial pressure. However, as he navigates through this challenging period, he discovers resilience, inner strength, and the importance of perseverance.

With the support of friends, mentors, and his own determination, Rahul learns that failure is not the end but rather a stepping stone towards personal growth and success. Through poignant moments of introspection and inspiring encounters, "12th Fail" portrays a compelling narrative of resilience, hope, and the transformative power of overcoming obstacles.
